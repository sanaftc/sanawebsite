IGNITE

IGNITE Kazakhstan: Transforming FTC in Asia In December 2024, Winning Alliance teams Clueless and Texpand launched IGNITE Kazakhstan, bringing their successful mentorship program to Asia for the first time. Designed to empower rookie robotics teams, IGNITE connects them with expert mentors in engineering, programming, and management, fostering growth and success in FTC.
