FTC Into The Deep Game Explanation

FTC Into the Deep is the theme for the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) 2025 season. Teams design and build robots to compete in underwater-themed challenges. The game involves navigating a simulated deep-sea environment where robots must perform tasks like collecting objects, scoring points, and overcoming obstacles.
